“Adults Swim” is the seventh track on Ski Mask’s self-titled album, STOKELEY. Ski posted a video of himself freestyling the lyrics over an ukelele in August 2018, and the full song was released in late November 2018. The title references the adult-oriented programming block on the Cartoon Network programming show “Adult Swim” that broadcasts shows such as Rick and Morty, which he frequently references in his music.
